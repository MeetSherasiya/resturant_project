Paneer Butter Masala

This creamy and rich dish features cubes of paneer (cottage cheese) cooked in a tomato-based gravy with butter and cream. The dish is flavored with a blend of spices such as turmeric, cumin, and cinnamon, and is often garnished with fresh coriander.

Baingan Bharta

This Punjabi dish is made from mashed eggplant that is roasted over an open flame until soft, and then mixed with a blend of spices and herbs. The dish is typically served with roti or naan, and can be made spicy or mild, depending on personal taste.

Masala Dosa

This South Indian specialty is a thin, crispy pancake made from a batter of rice and lentils, filled with a spiced potato filling, and served with coconut chutney and sambar. The dosa is traditionally cooked on a griddle, and is a popular breakfast dish across India.

Aloo Gobi

This vegetarian dish features chunks of potato (aloo) and cauliflower (gobi) cooked in a blend of spices such as turmeric, cumin, and coriander. The dish is often served with naan or roti and can be made spicy or mild, depending on personal taste.

Pav Bhaji

This Mumbai street food favorite consists of a spicy vegetable curry made with a blend of mashed vegetables such as potatoes, tomatoes, and peas, served with buttered bread rolls (pav). The curry is seasoned with a blend of spices such as coriander, cumin, and red chili powder.
